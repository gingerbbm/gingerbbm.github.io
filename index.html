KTB WTF?

Know The Board is an exercise aid for guitarists learning the notes on the fretboard, inspired by Mike Outram.

The exercise is simply to play every note on every string from fret 1 to fret 12 at a speed that suits you.

As Know The Board gives you a note, play it on each of the strings (high to low) while the click keeps time.

The order of notes is randomised. Do it daily and you'll soon know the board!
